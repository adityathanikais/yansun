Good to know

Questions businesses ask

What's the payback period for commercial solar?
Typically 3–5 years, depending on your tariff, roof size, and how much of your load solar covers. Sites with higher commercial tariffs tend to pay back faster. After payback, the power is essentially free for 20+ more years.
What if my roof isn't big enough for my demand?
That's where open access and group captive come in: you source solar power from an off-site plant through the grid. We'll assess your demand and recommend the right mix of rooftop and off-site generation.
Do you handle the TANGEDCO and regulatory approvals?
Yes, end to end. We manage net metering, open-access applications, and the relevant TNERC/TANGEDCO paperwork as part of the project.
Can you maintain a plant we already have?
Absolutely. Our O&M services cover existing plants too: monitoring, cleaning, fault resolution, and performance optimisation to recover lost output.
