Good to know

Questions, answered

The things people ask us most before going solar. Anything else, just message us on WhatsApp.

How much can I really save on my electricity bill?
A well-sized rooftop system can offset most or all of your daytime usage, cutting bills by up to 90% depending on your TANGEDCO tariff slab and consumption. Use the calculator above for a quick estimate, and we'll confirm exact figures on a free site visit.
What subsidy am I eligible for?
Homes get the central PM Surya Ghar subsidy, up to ₹78,000 for systems of 3 kW and above, and we file the entire application for you, from registration to disbursement. Subsidies apply to residential rooftop systems; businesses instead benefit from accelerated depreciation.
How long does installation take?
The physical install of a home system is typically completed in a few days. The longer part is utility approval and net-meter installation by TANGEDCO, which we coordinate end to end. We keep you updated at every stage.
Will solar work during power cuts and monsoon?
A standard grid-tied system pauses during a grid outage for safety; add battery storage if backup matters to you, and we'll size it. Panels still generate on cloudy and rainy days, just less, and your annual estimate already accounts for Tamil Nadu's weather.
What happens after installation? Am I on my own?
No. We monitor and maintain what we install, with long-term O&M to keep generation high. We've been a family-run business since 1999, so the team that builds your system is the same team you'll reach years later.
Do you handle large commercial and MW-scale projects too?
Yes. Alongside homes, we design and build commercial rooftop, open-access, and megawatt utility plants, including for state and union-territory governments across India. Tell us your scale and we'll propose the right ownership model.
How does solar actually lower my electricity bill?
Through net metering. During the day your panels power your home or business first, and any surplus is exported to the grid for credits on your TANGEDCO account. At night you draw those credits back, so your bill reflects only the net, which for a well-sized system is a fraction of what you pay today.
Do you offer financing or EMI options?
Yes. Solar can be paid upfront or funded through a bank loan, and many rooftop systems qualify for preferential solar financing. Tell us your situation and we'll point you to the current options, so your monthly saving can offset much of the instalment.
What panels and equipment do you use?
ALMM-listed, Tier-1 panels and reputed inverters: the same institutional-grade equipment we install on government and megawatt projects. We'll share the exact make and warranty for what we propose, so you know precisely what's going on your roof.
